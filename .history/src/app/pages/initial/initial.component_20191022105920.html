Our Work Flow

VautID is a solution that allows customers request for either prepaid or debit cards from banks. This a data capture system plus identity management system. It captures both the signature of customers and pictures as part of the criteria for requesting new cards. It eases off the stress of going to a bank to apply for bank cards, it brings the banks closer to the customer and ensures the smooth request and processing of bank cards.

How It Works

Request Card.

Request for card to be produced and/or personalized with informtation needed to be stored on card.

Card Informtation Imprint.

Processed card now contains all infomration required for payment services, identification, access to authorized places, depending on use cases.

Card Reader Scans Card.

Present card at point of authorization, place on device reader for card authorization verification.

Data Read.

Device reads information stored on card, verifies that with information available on database .

Informtation Search.

Device searches database and returns a match if information found or otherwise..

Confirms Identity.

Confirms the card owner information is present on database and allows access.
